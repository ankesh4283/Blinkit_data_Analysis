# 🛒 Blinkit Grocery – Revenue Intelligence & Market Performance Analytics  

## 📌 SQL-Based Business Intelligence Project  

An end-to-end analytics project focused on transforming raw retail data into actionable revenue intelligence, enabling data-driven decisions around outlet strategy, market expansion, and product positioning using PostgreSQL (SQL).

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement  

Retail businesses often struggle to answer:

- Which outlet types actually drive revenue?
- Are larger stores more profitable?
- Which locations should we expand into?
- What products do customers truly prefer?

This project answers these questions using structured data analysis.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective  

To build a data-driven framework that identifies:

- Revenue concentration across outlet types  
- Performance differences by outlet size  
- Location-based demand trends  
- Customer product preferences  
- Scalable business opportunities  

---

## 📊 Executive Summary  

- 💰 ₹12L+ Total Revenue Analyzed  
- 🏪 Multiple Outlet Types & Sizes Evaluated  
- 🌍 Tier-based Market Segmentation  
- 📦 Product-Level Insights Extracted  
- 📈 Revenue Contribution & Performance Benchmarked  

---

## 🛠 Analytical Approach  

### 🔹 Data Preparation  

- Cleaned and validated dataset  
- Checked null values and inconsistencies  
- Removed duplicate records using window functions  
- Ensured proper data types for analysis  

---

### 🔹 KPI Engineering (SQL)  

Developed key business metrics using:

- Aggregations: SUM(), COUNT()  
- Segmentation: GROUP BY  
- Window Functions: SUM() OVER()  
- Contribution Analysis: Revenue %  

---

### 🔹 Core Metrics Built  

- Total Revenue  
- Revenue % Contribution  
- Outlet Size Performance  
- Outlet Type Performance  
- Location Tier Analysis  
- Product Preference Distribution  

---

# 📈 Key Business Insights  

## 🏆 Medium Outlets – Revenue Sweet Spot  

- 42.27% of Total Revenue (Highest Contribution)

**Business Insight:**  
Medium-sized outlets outperform both small and large formats, indicating optimal efficiency.

---

## 🥈 Tier 3 Cities – Top Performing Markets  

- 39.29% Revenue Contribution (Highest)

**Business Insight:**  
Smaller cities are driving the majority of revenue, showing strong untapped potential.

---

## 🥉 Supermarket Type1 – Revenue Dependency Risk  

- 65.54% of Total Sales  

**Business Insight:**  
Revenue is heavily concentrated in one outlet type, creating potential risk.

---

## 🥗 Customer Preference – Low Fat Products  

- Low Fat: 64.6%  
- Regular: 35.4%  

**Business Insight:**  
Customers prefer healthier product options.

---

## 📊 Revenue Concentration Analysis  

- Top outlet type contributes 65%+ of total revenue  
- Medium + Small outlets contribute ~79% combined revenue  
- Large outlets contribute only ~20%  

**Insight:**  
Bigger stores do not guarantee higher performance.

---

## 🏙 Market Performance Insights  

- Tier 3 > Tier 2 > Tier 1 in revenue generation  
- Large outlets show lower performance  
- Sales distribution is highly concentrated  

---

## 💼 Strategic Recommendations  

- Expand medium-sized outlet model  
- Increase focus on Tier 3 markets  
- Reduce dependency on a single outlet type  
- Improve performance of large outlets  
- Expand low-fat product offerings
